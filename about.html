welcome

Beauty with Purpose Academy (Bewip), under FeNdo, transforms Eswatini by tackling youth unemployment, economic challenges, and gender inequality.

Bewip's model blends social investment and commercial viability, partnering with nonprofits and CSR-backed corporations to sponsor marginalized candidates while enrolling non-sponsored students for financial sustainability. This dual approach empowers economically challenged individuals and secures the academy's viability. Central to Bewip's mission is nurturing entrepreneurship and psychosocial well-being, equipping students with vital skills for self-sufficiency. Recognizing psychosocial challenges, especially for survivors of gender-based violence, Bewip integrates support into its curriculum, aiming to holistically empower individuals for success amid high youth unemployment and limited educational opportunities.
