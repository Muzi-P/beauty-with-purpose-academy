Learn About Us

We Have 4+ Years Of Experience

Beauty with Purpose Academy (Bewip), a subsidiary of FeNdo Group, strives to lead in beauty education, fostering passionate, self-sufficient professionals committed to excellence

In 2019, Bewip launched a pilot empowering women aged 21-35 from disadvantaged backgrounds with skills in beauty, fashion, entrepreneurship, and life. Partnering with donors, the four-month program impacted ten women in Manzini, covering modules like nail tech, makeup, wig making, and business. Nine graduates demonstrated successful execution and provided valuable insights for scaling the academy's impact.

Why Choose Us

Featured Questions

Bewip stands out with a unique blend of beauty education and social impact. We prioritize empowerment, especially for women in marginalized backgrounds, ensuring our students contribute to positive change while receiving top-tier beauty training

At Bewip, we go beyond traditional beauty training. Our graduates receive post-graduation support, including a beauty starter pack and mentorship. This comprehensive approach equips them with the skills and guidance needed to thrive in the beauty industry.

Bewip is synonymous with practical, real-world skills. Our hands-on training approach ensures that students not only gain theoretical knowledge but also acquire the practical expertise needed to excel in the competitive beauty industry

Our commitment to small classes provides personalized attention. The inclusive and community-focused approach ensures a supportive learning environment. At Bewip, we prioritize each student's success, creating an atmosphere where learning is enjoyable and impactful.
